## Summary
This was a marathon session that transformed the trading-system repo into a full personal AI assistant. Starting with research into Karpathy's LLM Wiki architecture and AI agent assistant patterns, I used Claude Code's new ultraplan feature to draft the system design in the cloud, then executed it with 4 parallel Coder agents creating 34 files across 6 new directories.

The assistant system has three layers: mutable daily state (todos, schedule, context, inbox), a long-term LLM wiki (Karpathy's three-layer pattern: raw sources, wiki pages, schema), and 6 specialized agents (briefer, scheduler, wiki-curator, inbox-processor, weekly-review, context-switcher). Work and home contexts are separated with recurring task awareness — the briefer knows about daily vehicle updates, Monday diesel vs hydrogen reports, and monthly invoice emails.

The most satisfying part was the wiki ingest. 74 personal memo files spanning 2023-2026 — a mix of LDS religious reflections, a complete cosmological theory, AI-gospel connections, and life philosophy — were categorized by topic and synthesized into 15 wiki pages with footnote citations back to every source file. Opening this in Obsidian immediately shows a web of interconnected ideas. I also set up Claude Code on an old Samsung Galaxy S20 FE via Termux + proot-distro Ubuntu, running Sonnet for quick assistant tasks. The Discord bot connection is 90% there — the server connects but MCP wiring needs a wrapper script fix, to be completed tomorrow.
